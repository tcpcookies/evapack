About EVA Pack

Your trusted EVA case manufacturing partner since 2006

Our Story

Founded in 2006, EVA Pack (Shenzhen) Co., Ltd. has grown from a small workshop into a leading EVA case manufacturer with a 4,800 m² state-of-the-art production facility in Huizhou, Guangdong.

With over 20 years of hands-on experience, we have perfected every step of the EVA case manufacturing process — from mould design and material selection to lamination, heat molding, stitching, and quality inspection. Everything happens under one roof.

Today, we serve clients across the globe, providing custom OEM/ODM EVA case solutions for industries ranging from consumer electronics and professional tools to medical devices and luxury watches.

Our commitment to quality is backed by international certifications including REACH, RoHS, CE, and CA Prop 65 — ensuring every product meets the highest global standards.
